Dashboard Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a deep dive into the operational and sales metrics of a pizza retail business. It analyzes 499 orders to uncover trends that help in stock optimization and staff scheduling.

1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales: $8,436
- Total Orders: 499
- Total Quantity Sold: 511 items

2. Sales by Category & Size
- Categories: The "Classic" category leads with 149 orders, followed by Chicken (134) and Supreme (122).
- Size Distribution: Large (L) pizzas are the most popular, accounting for 48% of total sales. Medium (M) follows at 30%.

3. Top 10 Best Sellers
- The "Classic Deluxe Pizza" is the top-performing product with 30 units sold.
- Other high-performers include the Pepperoni Pizza (26) and Four Cheese Pizza (23).

4. Time-Based Trends
- Peak Hours: The busiest times are during lunch (12 PM - 1 PM) and the dinner rush (6 PM - 7 PM).
- Monthly Activity: High engagement is seen in September (54 orders) and May (51 orders).

5. Interactive Features
- The dashboard allows filtering by Pizza Category, Pizza Size, and Specific Order Dates to see granular performance data.
